WELCOME!

"ANI BR" is place where we talk about Brazilin culture as it appears in different animes, organized by genres.

Japaneseculture is fascinating, and it becomes even more interesting when it is connectec with another culture, as happens in some anime that portray elements from different regions of Brazil. Although nowadays Brazil is often represented through stereotypes in anime, it is still interesting to observe how elements such as capoeira, caipirinhas, and even Brazilian volleyball are represented and érceived through the eyes of people from other countries.

WARNING: THIS CONTENT CONTAINS SPOILERS FOR ALL THE ANIME MENTIONED!!

On this website, we will present representations and homages found in the following animes:

Sports: Haikyuu, Inazuma Eleven and Blue Lock;

Science Fiction and Crime: Cowboy Bebop, Odd Taxi and Michiko e Hatchin;

Slice of life: Skull-face bookseller Honda-san and Nare Nare cheer for you.

This website was created with the purpose of demonstrating comprehension skills related to the course "Methods and Techniques Applied to Multilingualism (MTAM) from the Applied Foreign Languages (LEA-MSI) course, the second semester of 2025.
